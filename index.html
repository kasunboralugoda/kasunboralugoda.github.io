Care Settings Experience

Care Homes

Residential and nursing care environments.

I have experience supporting individuals in residential and nursing care settings, focusing on dignity, safety, and wellbeing. My work includes assisting with daily living activities, following care plans, and working closely with senior carers and nursing staff to provide consistent, person-centred care.

Domiciliary Care

Supporting individuals in their own homes.

I support individuals in their own homes to live independently and safely, providing one-to-one care tailored to their needs. This includes assisting with daily routines, offering medication prompts, and respecting privacy, choice, and independence in a familiar home environment.

Day Centres

Community-based support and engagement.

I have experience supporting people in day centre settings by encouraging social interaction, participation in group activities, and emotional wellbeing. My role involves helping individuals stay engaged, build confidence, and take part in meaningful daily activities within the community.
